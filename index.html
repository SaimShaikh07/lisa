Who is lisa?

Lalisa Manoban, better known by her stage name Lisa, is a Thai rapper, singer, and dancer. She was born on March 27, 1997, in Buriram Province, Thailand, and raised in Bangkok. Lisa is of Thai descent, but she also has some Chinese ancestry.



Before joining BLACKPINK, Lisa was a trainee at YG Entertainment for four years. She was the only non-Korean member of the group and had to learn the Korean language and culture to fit in. Despite the language and cultural barriers, Lisa quickly became a fan favorite with her unique style and charming personality.
